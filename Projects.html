项目与合作 (Projects)

Our lab focuses on understanding how marine and freshwater diatoms adapt and evolve in response to environmental changes, using integrated physiological, biochemical, and transcriptomic approaches.
我们实验室致力于揭示海洋与淡水硅藻在环境变化下的适应与进化机制，结合生理、生化及转录组学等多层次研究手段。

1. Long-term Experimental Evolution / 长期实验进化

We conduct long-term culturing experiments (over hundreds of generations) under varying temperatures and light regimes to explore how diatoms evolve thermal tolerance and optimize metabolic efficiency under sustained stress.
通过在不同温度和光照条件下进行数百代培养实验，研究硅藻在持续环境压力下如何进化获得耐热性并优化代谢效率。

2. Transcriptome–Physiology Coupling / 转录组–生理耦合

We integrate transcriptomic data with physiological measurements to link gene expression modules with photosynthetic performance, growth rate, and biochemical composition.
将转录组数据与光合生理、生长速率和生化组成等指标耦合，解析基因表达调控网络与功能性状之间的对应关系。

3. Mechanisms of Environmental Adaptation / 环境适应机制

We investigate how diatoms respond to multifactorial stresses (temperature, light, nutrients, UV-B, desiccation) and identify key regulatory pathways that enable adaptation at molecular and cellular levels.
研究硅藻对多重环境因子的响应（温度、光照、营养、紫外辐射、干旱等），识别其在分子与细胞水平实现环境适应的关键调控机制。

4. Evolutionary Genomics and Gene Age / 进化基因组与基因年龄

By combining comparative genomics and transcriptome evolution analyses, we explore how gene age, duplication, and polyploidization contribute to diatom adaptation and speciation.
结合比较基因组与转录组进化分析，揭示基因年龄、重复与多倍化在硅藻适应与物种形成过程中的作用。
