项目与合作 (Projects)

Our laboratory is dedicated to systematically elucidating the adaptation mechanisms and evolutionary patterns of marine diatoms under environmental changes by integrating multi-level research approaches, including physiology, biochemistry, and omics. At the same time, we actively conduct a series of application-oriented research on algal resource development, aiming to translate fundamental scientific discoveries into practical applications.
我们实验室致力于通过整合生理、生化及组学等多层次研究手段，系统解析海洋硅藻在环境变化下的适应机制与进化规律。同时，我们也积极开展一系列面向应用的藻类资源开发研究，旨在推动基础科学研究成果向实际应用转化。

1. 基于多倍化的海洋硅藻暖化适应研究，探索其在长期升温环境下的基因组、生理与生态响应机制，为预测气候变化下海洋浮游植物群落的演化趋势提供理论依据。（ Research on the adaptation of marine diatoms to warming based on polyploidy, exploring their genomic, physiological, and ecological response mechanisms under long-term warming environments, to provide a theoretical basis for predicting the evolutionary trends of marine phytoplankton communities under climate change.）
国家自然科学基金项目:升温诱导假微型海链藻基因组多倍体化的功能与进化机制研究(2021-2023)
国家自然科学基金项目:基于实验进化的海洋多倍化硅藻长期暖化下的适应性进化研究(2024-2027)

2.微藻生物肥料研发与应用：针对农业生产需求，系统开展微藻肥的规模化制备工艺、养分释放机理及作物施用效应研究，推动其在绿色农业与可持续土壤改良中的实践应用。( Research and Application of Microalgal Biofertilizers: To address the needs of agricultural production, this study systematically investigates the large-scale production processes, nutrient release mechanisms, and crop application effects of microalgal fertilizers, promoting their practical use in green agriculture and sustainable soil improvement.)
陕西省科技厅重点研发计划项目:新型蓝藻肥关键技术研发(2022–2023)
西安市科技局项目：冬小麦专用微藻肥的研发和示范(2025-2027)
