研究方向 (Research)

实验室简介 | About the Lab

硅藻进化与适应实验室（DEAL）主要关注硅藻在全球变化背景下的生态适应与进化机制研究。我们通过多代进化实验、转录组学、代谢组学及生态生理学方法，揭示硅藻在不同温度、光照和养分条件下的长期适应过程与分子调控机制。
The Diatom Evolution & Adaptation Lab (DEAL) focuses on understanding the ecological adaptation and evolutionary mechanisms of diatoms under global environmental change. Using long-term evolution experiments, transcriptomics, metabolomics, and eco-physiological approaches, we investigate how diatoms adapt to variations in temperature, light, and nutrient availability across generations.

主要研究方向 | Research Focus

  • 硅藻对温度变化的长期适应与快速进化机制
    Long-term and rapid adaptation of diatoms to temperature changes
  • 环境胁迫（光照、营养盐、铁限制）下的分子响应网络
    Molecular regulatory networks under environmental stresses (light, nutrients, iron)
  • 高原及极端环境硅藻的生态适应与进化
    Adaptation and evolution of diatoms from high-altitude and extreme environments
