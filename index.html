实验室简介 | About the Lab

硅藻生态、适应与进化实验室（DEAEL）于2021年4月在西安成立，主要致力于揭示硅藻在全球变化背景下的生态适应与进化机制。 实验室综合运用实验进化、生理生化分析、多组学整合及生态学研究等多学科手段，系统探讨硅藻在温度、光照、营养等环境变化下的适应和进化规律与分子调控机制。我们的研究旨在连接外部环境变化与硅藻内部适应过程之间的联系，从微观分子机制到宏观生态响应，揭示其进化与适应的统一规律。
The Diatom Ecology, Adaptation and Evolution Laboratory (DEAEL) was established in April 2021 in Xi’an, China. Our research aims to elucidate the ecological adaptation and evolutionary mechanisms of diatoms under the context of global environmental change. By integrating experimental evolution, physiological and biochemical analyses, multi-omics approaches, and ecological investigations, we explore how diatoms adapt and evolved to variations in temperature, light, and nutrient conditions. Our work seeks to bridge the gap between external environmental shifts and the intrinsic adaptive responses of diatoms, revealing the unified principles that link molecular regulation with ecological and evolutionary outcomes.

2025-10-23硅藻长期适应研究成果发表于 Nature Climate Change / Polyploidization in diatoms accelerates adaptation to warming

我们揭示了海洋硅藻通过多倍化来快速适应暖化，为理解硅藻的暖化适应提供新视角。

The authors obtained large-volume individuals of diatom cultures under thermal stress. These polyploids (having more than two sets of chromosomes) are shown to rapidly adapt to high temperatures, highlighting polyploidization as a possible adaptive measure for diatoms under climate change.
